"Angel-A" is a 2005 French film directed by Luc Besson. In the main roles we see Jamel Debbouze as André and Rie Rasmussen as Angela. The film is about André, a petty criminal in Paris, who is deeply in debt and sees no other way out than to take his own life. Standing on the Pont Alexandre III to jump, he meets Angela, a mysterious and beautiful woman who is also about to take her own life. Together they go through a series of events where Angela helps André find the strength to change his life. The film explores themes of forgiveness, self-acceptance and love, with Paris as a beautiful and black-and-white backdrop.
